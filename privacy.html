Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

✅ No data collected · No tracking · No ads

1 Our Commitment to Privacy

Nepal Sky is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how our service works with respect to your data.

2 Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Nepal Sky operates without user accounts, cookies, tracking mechanisms, or analytics. You can use the entire service anonymously.

3 Location Data

When you use the "Use My Current Location" feature, your browser's geolocation API provides your coordinates. This data:

  • Is processed entirely within your browser
  • Is never transmitted to our servers
  • Is only used to fetch weather data for your location
  • Is not stored or logged

4 Third-Party Services

Nepal Sky uses the following third-party APIs to provide weather data:

  • Open-Meteo — Free, open-source weather API for real-time city weather data
  • Department of Hydrology and Meteorology (DHM), Nepal — for official weather analysis and province forecasts
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — for location search functionality

These services may have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5 Local Storage

Nepal Sky uses your browser's localStorage to save your last searched location, recent search history, and language preference. This data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere. You can clear it anytime using the refresh button on the dashboard or by clearing your browser data.

6 No Cookies

Nepal Sky does not use any cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

7 No Advertising

Nepal Sky is completely ad-free. We do not display advertisements or use advertising networks.

8 Children's Privacy

Nepal Sky does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10 Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at: hello@laxman-poudel.com.np
